    Description

    Mr Clope is known for his firm belief that new coding languages ​​are just passing and unnecessary fads, and he refuses to use any of them.   • Jubiléia

    Description

    A young developer in training, Jubiléia already stands out for her peculiarity: a declared aversion to clear themes in programming environments. She has a strong preference for dark themes, which she finds softer and easier on her eyes, especially during long coding sessions at night.

  • Solar Storm

    Description

    In the developer universe, Solar Storm is known as one of the most formidable villains. It has the power to generate massive solar flares that disrupt communication networks, affecting the internet and all connected devices. These solar flares cause chaos in the digital world, destabilizing servers, disrupting connections and causing loss of critical data.
